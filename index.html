About Me

I am a scientist who has spent most of her graduate school time researching spectroscopy of solar-energy materials, before finding herself increasingly passionate about chemistry education research. I am currently a postdoctoral fellow at Humboldt University of Berlin (Tiemann Chemistry Education Lab), creating and evaluating digital learning environments for chemistry. This website was initially created to host my teaching portfolio as part of the Delta program at UW-Madison, but it is also a starting point linking to my research and other professional projects.
